ABOUT CLASSICAL PARADISE





The Inception of Classical paradise
Classical paradise has been designed and built by architectural experts from Indonesia who have also worked on several well known & noteworthy resorts in many countries including India. Backed by investments from Singapore, this hotel-resort is an infusion of contemporary architecture with a hint of naturality. Bespoke of contemporary impressions, Classical paradise is built to look like boxes stacked together. However, the main components of this building are terracotta, exposed bricks, sandstone, and wooden louvre. This not only creates a great ambience but also facilitates a touch with nature. The appearance of Classical paradise makes it unique and iconic in the locality while providing guests with great hospitality and a homely feeling. Enjoy an Unwinding Stay in Bengaluru Guests who stay at Classical paradise Hotel, Bengaluru, can choose from 7 well-appointed accommodations.




Why choose Classical paradise?
The Classical paradise Hotel, located at Bengaluru, is one of the most luxurious and beautiful hotels in the entire city and that was a five-star hotel with all the modern features. Most of the modern facilities were accumulated at the hotel for its clients. The services were up to the mark while the accommodations system appeared to be one of the bests. The hotel is always crowded with visitors and most of them are tourists from different countries. The environment is friendly and warm and the treatment as a guest or as an individual is adorable indeed. The hotel is a business run by the family members and thus the values of the noble family are represented by the staffs and crews.The hotel, contained almost all the modern amenities. The entire hotel building was covered with the central cooling system. The lobby, the bar, the restaurants etc. were nicely decorated. The rooms were top class with some exceptional and stunning furniture. The staffs and crew members were genuinely cordial with their service.The advisory body for the hotels always tries to reflect the attitudes of the family and it appears the nobility of the family is worth admiration for the visitors. Moreover, the hotel is located in the centre of the Bengaluru city and thus it is accessible through different vehicles and routes. The hotel has its own spacious parking facility where the visitors could park their vehicles comfortably.
